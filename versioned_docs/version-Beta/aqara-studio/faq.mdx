# FAQ

## What to do if I cannot log in to Aqara Studio?

If you are unable to log in to Aqara Studio, please follow these troubleshooting steps:

1. **Check whether the account exists:**
    - If you do not have an account, please contact a user with super administrator permissions to create one for you.
    - If you already have an account, refer to the following information to recall your username and password:
        - Common usernames: `super`, `admin`, `operator` or other custom names you may have used.
        - Password requirements: 8-16 characters, must include uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

2. **If you still cannot log in and cannot remember your password, please choose one of the following solutions based on the deployment model of Aqara Studio:**
    - **Hub-Integrated Model**: Please refer to the device manual to perform a factory reset.
    - **Edge-Deployed Model**: Please uninstall the current Aqara Studio and reinstall it.

    :::tip
    Factory reset will clear all data.
    :::
