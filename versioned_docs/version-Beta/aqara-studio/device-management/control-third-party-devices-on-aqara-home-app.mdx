# Control Third Party Devices on Aqara Home App

:::tip
This feature is only available for the **hub-integrated** version of Aqara Studio.
:::

This guide will walk you through the process of integrating third-party devices into the Aqara Home App, enabling efficient and unified management of smart home devices using different protocols within the same space.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, please make sure you have integrated third-party devices into Aqara Studio by following the relevant guides below:
- [Home Assistant](./../device-integration/home-assistant.mdx)
- [Modbus TCP](./../device-integration/modbus-tcp.mdx)
- [BACnet](./../device-integration/bacnet.mdx)
- [KNX IP](./../device-integration/knx-ip.mdx)
- [MQTT](./../device-integration/mqtt.mdx)

## Process

1. Go to the **Devices** page, and click **View all devices** in the **Device Tree**.
2. In the **Device List**, find the target device, click the **...** button, and from the dropdown menu select **Connect to Aqara Home**.
3. Open the Aqara Home App, go to the **Accessories** tab, and you can view and control the newly-connected device.
