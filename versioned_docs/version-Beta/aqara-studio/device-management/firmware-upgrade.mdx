# Firmware Upgrade

This article introduces how to upgrade the firmware of Aqara devices (Aqara Hub, Aqara Modbus, Zigbee, Matter) via Aqara Studio.

## Steps


1. In the left sidebar, click **Devices**, and select the desired Aqara device's protocol (Aqara Hub, Aqara Modbus, Zigbee, Matter) in the device tree. On the **Protocol Detail** page, click **Firmware Upgrade**.

    ![Click the Firmware Upgrade button](https://itad-aqara-docs-1300889962.aqara.com/dev/img/aqara-studio/en/FirmwareUpgrade-ClickButton.png)

2. Select the devices you want to upgrade, then click the **Start Upgrade** button to begin the upgrade process.
