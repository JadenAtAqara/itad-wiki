# Energy Management System

## Overview

Aqara Studio’s energy management system can automatically collect data from all types of energy-consuming devices, supporting daily, weekly, and monthly multi-dimensional statistics and trend analysis. Through this UI, you can intuitively view the total energy usage for your entire space, rank energy consumption by space, device, or device type, and analyze specific energy usage trends in depth.

This feature helps you monitor the energy consumption status of various areas and devices in real time, quickly identify high-consumption devices and abnormal usage behavior, and provides decision support for energy-saving optimization and efficiency management.

Additionally, the energy management dashboard presents energy data visually and centrally, providing strong data support for subsequent efficiency analysis, energy consumption warnings, and device maintenance.

## Prerequisites

The energy management page displays only devices capable of reporting power consumption data (such as refrigerators, air conditioners, switches, sockets, and other devices with energy statistics capability), and only statistics based on each device's **Cumulative Energy Consumption** data point are collected.

Energy management can display energy data from different protocol devices, but specific visibility depends on the device protocol configuration:

- **Aqara** devices and those in the **Home Assistant** ecosystem natively support this feature. As long as they are connected to Aqara Studio, their data is shown in the energy management page directly, with no extra adjustment required.
- For devices using **other protocols**, you must manually map the energy consumption-related data point to **Cumulative Energy Consumption** when integrating them, otherwise Aqara Studio will not be able to retrieve their energy data.

:::tip
If you want to monitor energy consumption for devices that do not support energy statistics by themselves (such as lights), you can connect them to a smart socket or smart meter, then view the energy data for that socket or meter in the energy management page to indirectly obtain the device’s consumption.
:::

## Adjust Data

Go to **EMS** from the left sidebar. By default, all devices in all spaces are shown. No setup is required.

If you need custom data, see the settings table below:

| Component | Description | Configuration |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Energy Data** | <ul><li><strong>Total Energy</strong>: Total energy usage in the last 365 days</li><li><strong>Today's Energy</strong>: From 00:00 to now, updated hourly</li><li><strong>Yesterday's Energy</strong>: Total energy for the previous day</li><li><strong>7-Day Energy</strong>: Cumulative energy for the last 7 days</li><li><strong>30-Day Energy</strong>: Cumulative energy for the last 30 days</li></ul> | <ul><li><strong>Device Data Source</strong>: Choose device scope (all/specified)</li><li><strong>Sub Data Display</strong>: Independently display Today, Yesterday, 7-Day, and 30-Day energy consumption</li></ul> |
| **Energy Trends** | Line chart shows energy changes over time, identifying consumption peaks and valleys | <ul><li><strong>Custom Name</strong>: Customize chart name</li><li><strong>Time Unit</strong>: hour, day, week, month</li><li><strong>Period</strong>: Custom time range</li><li><strong>Device Data Source</strong>: Choose device scope (all/specified)</li><li><strong>Statistic Dimension</strong>: Show max/min values for the selected period</li></ul> |
| **Energy Top by Space** | Pie chart shows energy proportion by space (building, floor, room), helping locate high-consumption areas | <ul><li><strong>Custom Name</strong>: Customize chart name</li><li><strong>Time Unit</strong>: hour, day, week, month</li><li><strong>Period</strong>: Custom time range</li><li><strong>Space Data Source</strong>: All spaces/specified spaces</li></ul> |
| **Energy Top by Device** | Ranks energy-consuming devices to quickly locate primary consumers | <ul><li><strong>Custom Name</strong>: Customize chart name</li><li><strong>Time Unit</strong>: hour, day, week, month</li><li><strong>Period</strong>: Custom time range</li><li><strong>Device Data Source</strong>: Top 5 / Top 10 / All devices / Specified devices</li></ul> |
| **Energy Trend by Device Type** | Shows energy trends by device type (e.g., lights, air conditioners), analyzing overall usage changes by type | <ul><li><strong>Custom Name</strong>: Customize chart name</li><li><strong>Time Unit</strong>: hour, day, week, month</li><li><strong>Period</strong>: Custom time range</li><li><strong>Device Type</strong>: All device types / specified device types</li><li><strong>Statistic Dimension</strong>: Show max/min values for the selected period</li></ul> |
| **Energy MoM** | Shows week-over-week energy stats for intuitive comparison of increases/decreases | <ul><li><strong>Custom Name</strong>: Customize chart name</li><li><strong>Time Unit</strong>: week</li><li><strong>Device Data Source</strong>: Choose device scope (all/specified)</li></ul> |
| **Energy Consumption Calendar** | Calendar view of daily energy with colored levels for historical review and periodic analysis | <ul><li><strong>Custom Name</strong>: Customize chart name</li><li><strong>Target Month</strong>: Freely select month to query</li><li><strong>Device Data Source</strong>: Choose device scope (all/specified)</li><li><strong>Energy Level Config</strong>: Customize value range and color for each consumption level</li></ul> |
