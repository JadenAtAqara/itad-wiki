# Compatible Aqara Devices

Aqara Studio supports access to and management of a wide range of Aqara smart devices, including but not limited to sensors, switches, lighting controls, gateways, and other product categories.

For the most up-to-date and complete list of compatible devices, please read the [[Aqara Studio] Supported Device List&Function](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1wqkp7AESs7P3zrgKu9nWO8ZSL-uo3iKFdborJxRxUq4/edit?usp=sharing). This online document continuously updates and covers all supported device models, helping you quickly understand product support details.
