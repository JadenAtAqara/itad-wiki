# Apple Home

Connecting to Apple Home ecosystem.

#### Matter

0. Please make sure you have installed the Apple Home app and have a Thread-enabled Matter Controller of Apple.
1. After the device is powered on, the indicator light will begin to flash slowly, indicating that the device has entered network setting mode.
2. Open the Apple Home app, scan the Matter QR code on the instruction manual or product, or enter the setup code to add the device to the app.

#### Homekit

0. Please make sure you have installed the Apple Home app.
1. After the device is powered on, the indicator light will begin to flash slowly, indicating that the device has entered network setting mode.
2. Open the Apple Home app, scan the HoemKit QR code on the instruction manual or product, or enter the setup code to add the device to the app.

#### Thread-enabled Matter Controller

- Apple HomePod (second-gen)
- Apple HomePod Mini
- Apple TV 4K (2nd gen)
- Apple TV 4K (3rd gen, 128 GB)
